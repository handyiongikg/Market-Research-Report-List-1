<p><strong>The Silicon-Based Anode Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SiO/C</li><li>Si/C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon-based anode material market is primarily categorized into two types: SiO/C (silicon oxide/carbon) and Si/C (silicon/carbon). SiO/C combines silicon oxide with carbon to enhance conductivity and stability, making it suitable for lithium-ion batteries. On the other hand, Si/C utilizes silicon mixed with carbon, allowing for higher capacity and improved cycling performance. Both types address the growing demand for efficient, high-energy-density battery solutions in electric vehicles and portable electronics, reflecting innovation in energy storage technology.</p></p>
