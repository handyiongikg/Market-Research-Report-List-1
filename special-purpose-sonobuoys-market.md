<p><p>The special-purpose sonobuoy market encompasses various types tailored for specific applications. Bathythermobuoys (BT) are designed for measuring underwater temperature and pressure profiles, aiding in submarine operations. Search and Rescue (SAR) buoys enhance maritime rescue efforts by providing location tracking and communication capabilities. Air Transportable Communication (ATAC) buoys facilitate reliable communication for tactical operations, while Down-link Communication (DLC) buoys transmit sonar data to surface vessels or aircraft, enhancing situational awareness and operational efficiency in naval missions.</p></p>
