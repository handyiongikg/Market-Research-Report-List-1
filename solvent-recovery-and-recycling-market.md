<p><p>The solvent recovery and recycling market is segmented into on-site and off-site processes. On-site solvent recycling involves the reuse and purification of solvents directly at the generating facility, promoting efficiency and reducing transportation costs. This method allows for immediate recovery and maximizes sustainability within the operation. Off-site solvent recycling, in contrast, involves collecting solvents from multiple facilities for processing at specialized plants. This approach benefits from economies of scale and advanced technologies, ensuring effective recovery and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
