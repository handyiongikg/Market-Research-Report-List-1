<p><p>The Titanium Nickel target market is divided into two main types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are typically stationary and are used in applications requiring uniform coating thickness, making them suitable for thin-film deposition. In contrast, rotating targets facilitate enhanced material utilization and uniformity in coatings due to their continuous movement during the deposition process. Both types are crucial in various industries, including electronics and optics, where precision and efficiency in thin-film production are essential.</p></p>
