<p><p>The Bluetooth Smart SoC market is categorized into two main types: Bluetooth Smart and Bluetooth Smart Ready. Bluetooth Smart devices, also known as Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), are optimized for low power consumption and are ideal for wearables, health monitors, and IoT applications. In contrast, Bluetooth Smart Ready devices can support both classic Bluetooth and Bluetooth Smart, offering broader compatibility and versatility, making them suitable for smartphones, tablets, and other multi-functional devices. This differentiation drives innovation and adoption across various industries.</p></p>
