<p><p>The SMD DC-DC converter market is categorized into isolated and non-isolated types. Isolated converters ensure electrical separation between input and output, enhancing safety and reducing noise, making them ideal for sensitive applications. Non-isolated converters, on the other hand, do not provide this separation, resulting in simpler designs and generally lower costs. They are commonly used for lower-power applications where size and efficiency are critical. Each type serves distinct applications based on power, size, and safety requirements.</p></p>
