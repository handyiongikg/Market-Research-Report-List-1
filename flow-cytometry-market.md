<p><strong>The Flow Cytometry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell-based</li><li>Bead-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flow cytometry market can be categorized into cell-based and bead-based segments. Cell-based flow cytometry primarily analyzes individual cells' physical and biochemical characteristics, allowing for detailed study of cell populations. In contrast, bead-based flow cytometry utilizes microspheres or beads to capture and analyze specific analytes such as proteins or nucleic acids, enabling multiplexed assays. Both types serve distinct yet complementary purposes in research, diagnostics, and therapeutic development by providing insights into cellular functions and biomolecular interactions.</p></p>
