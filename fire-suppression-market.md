<p><p>The fire suppression market includes various types, each designed for specific applications. Dry chemical fire suppression utilizes powdered agents to extinguish flames, effective for Class A, B, and C fires. Foam fire suppression employs foam agents to smother fires, particularly effective for flammable liquids. CO2 fire suppression uses carbon dioxide to displace oxygen, ideal for electrical and flammable material fires. Additionally, the "Others" segment encompasses innovative methods such as water mist and clean agents, catering to diverse fire scenarios.</p></p>
