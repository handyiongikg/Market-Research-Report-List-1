<p><p>The transparent acrylic sheets market comprises two main types: extruded acrylic sheets and cast acrylic sheets. Extruded acrylic sheets are produced through a continuous extrusion process, resulting in lighter, less expensive sheets with good optical clarity and impact resistance, commonly used in signage and displays. In contrast, cast acrylic sheets are created by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, offering superior clarity, thickness options, and UV resistance, making them ideal for applications like windows, aquariums, and high-end furniture.</p></p>
