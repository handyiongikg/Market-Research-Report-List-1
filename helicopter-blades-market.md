<p><p>The helicopter blades market is primarily divided into two main types: main rotor blades and tail rotor blades. Main rotor blades are critical for lift and flight control, affecting overall performance and stability. They are designed for various sizes and specifications to accommodate different helicopter models. Tail rotor blades, on the other hand, serve to counteract torque produced by the main rotor, ensuring directional control and stability. Both types play essential roles in the overall functionality and safety of helicopters.</p></p>
