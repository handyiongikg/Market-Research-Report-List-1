<p><p>Ladle furnaces are critical in steelmaking, utilized for refining molten metal before casting. The market comprises two main types: direct current (DC) furnaces and alternating current (AC) furnaces. DC furnaces offer superior control of temperature and composition, making them ideal for high-quality steel production. In contrast, AC furnaces provide a more cost-effective solution for producing larger volumes of steel and are widely used in electric arc steelmaking. Both types cater to diverse metallurgical needs in the industry.</p></p>
