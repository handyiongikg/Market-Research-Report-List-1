<p><p>The optical food sorting machines market features three main types: X-ray sorting systems, color sorting systems, and NIR sorting systems. X-ray sorting systems detect foreign materials and density variations, ensuring food safety and quality. Color sorting systems utilize advanced camera technology to identify and separate products based on color, enhancing quality control. NIR (Near-Infrared) sorting systems analyze the chemical composition of food items, allowing for the differentiation of products based on moisture and fat content, optimizing inventory and reducing waste.</p></p>
