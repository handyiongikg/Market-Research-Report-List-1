<p><p>The gate openers market is primarily divided into two types: sliding gate openers and swing gate openers. Sliding gate openers are designed for gates that slide along a track, making them ideal for properties with limited space. They are commonly used in commercial and residential settings. In contrast, swing gate openers pivot the gate outward or inward, suitable for sites with adequate space to accommodate the gate's arc. Both types offer automated solutions for enhanced security and convenience.</p></p>
