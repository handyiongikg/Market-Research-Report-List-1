<p><p>The tin containers market comprises various types, including built-up and shallow drawn containers. Built-up containers are assembled from multiple components, providing enhanced strength and customization for packaging needs. In contrast, shallow drawn containers are produced by stretching metal sheets into shallow shapes, ideal for products requiring less depth and more surface area, such as small food items. Both types cater to diverse industries, offering durability, lightweight options, and effective branding opportunities while ensuring product protection.</p></p>
