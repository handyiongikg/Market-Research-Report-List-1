<p><ul><li>Distributed Sensing</li><li>Fiber Optic Point Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic sensing solutions encompass two primary market types: distributed sensing and fiber optic point sensing. Distributed sensing utilizes continuous fiber optics along a length to monitor variables like temperature, strain, and pressure across large areas, making it ideal for applications in infrastructure and environmental monitoring. In contrast, fiber optic point sensing focuses on specific locations, providing precise measurements for localized conditions. Both types leverage the advantages of fiber optics, such as high sensitivity and immunity to electromagnetic interference.</p></p>
