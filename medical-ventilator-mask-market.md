<p><p>The medical ventilator mask market primarily includes two main types: oronasal masks and full face masks. Oronasal masks cover both the mouth and nose, providing effective ventilation while allowing for natural oral communication and easier insertion of feeding tubes. Full face masks encompass the entire face, ensuring a secure fit and minimizing air leaks, which is crucial for patients requiring high levels of oxygen support. Both types are essential for various respiratory therapies and critical care settings.</p></p>
