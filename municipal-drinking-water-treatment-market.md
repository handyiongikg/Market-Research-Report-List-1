<p><strong>The Municipal Drinking Water Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reverse Osmosis Membranes</li><li>Ion-Exchange</li><li>Ultrafiltration (UF) Membranes</li><li>Electrodeionization (EDI)</li><li>Clarification, Filtration and Chlorination</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The municipal drinking water treatment market includes several key technologies. Reverse osmosis membranes effectively remove contaminants through a semipermeable barrier. Ion-exchange processes replace undesirable ions with more desirable ones, improving water quality. Ultrafiltration (UF) membranes separate particles and microorganisms based on size, while electrodeionization (EDI) uses electrical currents to remove ions. Clarification and filtration help in sediment removal, ensuring clearer water. Chlorination is a disinfection method that eliminates pathogens, while "others" encompasses emerging treatments and technologies aimed at enhancing water safety and quality.</p></p>
