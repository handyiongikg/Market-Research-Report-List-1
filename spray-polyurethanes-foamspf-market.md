<p><p>Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) is a versatile insulation material used in various applications. The market is primarily categorized into open cell and closed cell types. Open cell foam is lightweight, flexible, and allows air to flow, making it ideal for interior applications and soundproofing. Closed cell foam is denser, offers superior insulation, and acts as a moisture barrier, suitable for external structures and roofs. Other market segments include specialty foams tailored for specific industrial uses, further broadening SPF applications.</p></p>
