<p><strong>The Coil Coating Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coil coating additives market is primarily categorized into solventborne and waterborne types. Solventborne additives utilize organic solvents to dissolve resins, offering excellent performance in terms of adhesion and durability, but may pose environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, waterborne additives are environmentally friendly, using water as a solvent to reduce VOC emissions. While they may exhibit lower performance in some applications compared to solventborne options, their growing popularity is driven by stricter environmental regulations and sustainability preferences.</p></p>
