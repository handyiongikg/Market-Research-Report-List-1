<p><p>The dental overdenture market consists of two main types: complete overdentures and removable partial overdentures. Complete overdentures are designed for patients with no remaining natural teeth, providing a stable and functional solution that sits over the gums, often retained by implants or natural tooth roots. Removable partial overdentures, on the other hand, cater to patients with some remaining natural teeth, offering a supplementary prosthetic that enhances aesthetics and function while allowing easy removal for cleaning and maintenance.</p></p>
