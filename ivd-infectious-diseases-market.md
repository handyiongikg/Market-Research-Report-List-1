Molecular diagnostics, on the other hand, uses techniques like PCR to identify pathogens by analyzing their genetic material. Both segments play crucial roles in disease detection and monitoring, enhancing clinical decision-making and patient management through accurate and timely infectious disease diagnosis.</p></p>
