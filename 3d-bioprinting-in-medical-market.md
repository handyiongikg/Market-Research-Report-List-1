<p><p>3D bioprinting in the medical market encompasses various advanced techniques: </p><p>1. **Magnetic 3D Bioprinting** uses magnetic fields to align and position cells for precise tissue structure.</p><p>2. **Laser-Assisted Bioprinting** employs laser energy to deposit bio-ink layers, enabling high-resolution patterns. </p><p>3. **Inkjet 3D Bioprinting** sprays bio-ink droplets to create complex tissue architectures. </p><p>4. **Microextrusion 3D Bioprinting** pushes bio-inks through fine nozzles, enabling continuous filament deposition for building tissue structures. Each method offers unique advantages for tissue engineering and regenerative medicine.</p></p>
