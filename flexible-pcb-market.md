<p><strong>The Flexible PCB Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Double Layer</li><li>Multi-Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible PCB market is categorized into three main types: Single Layer, Double Layer, and Multi-Layer PCBs. Single Layer PCBs consist of a single conductive layer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for basic applications. Double Layer PCBs include two conductive layers, allowing for more complex designs and improved functionality. Multi-Layer PCBs have three or more layers, enabling intricate circuitry and high-density components, making them ideal for advanced electronics in aerospace, medical, and consumer device applications. Each type serves specific needs based on complexity and performance requirements.</p></p>
