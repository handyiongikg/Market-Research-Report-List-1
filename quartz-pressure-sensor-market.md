<p><strong>The Quartz Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive Quartz Pressure Sensor</li><li>Piezoelectric Quartz Pressure Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quartz Pressure Sensor market features two main types: Piezoresistive and Piezoelectric Quartz Pressure Sensors. Piezoresistive sensors measure pressure by altering resistance in response to stress on a quartz element, offering high accuracy and stability in static pressure applications. In contrast, Piezoelectric sensors generate an electrical charge in response to applied pressure, making them ideal for dynamic measurements, such as vibrations or rapid pressure changes. Both sensor types are widely used in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and medical applications.</p></p>
