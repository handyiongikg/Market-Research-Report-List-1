<p><ul><li>No Wire Tube</li><li>Wire Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Drilling Hoses market is primarily categorized into two types: No Wire Tube and Wire Tube hoses. No Wire Tube hoses are constructed without internal wire reinforcements, making them lighter and more flexible, suitable for low-pressure applications. In contrast, Wire Tube hoses incorporate high-strength wire layers for added reinforcement, enabling them to withstand high pressures and harsh drilling conditions. Both types cater to different drilling needs, with Wire Tube hoses preferred for demanding scenarios, while No Wire options are advantageous for lighter tasks.</p></p>
