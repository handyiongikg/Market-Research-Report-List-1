<p><strong>The Optical Measurement Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMMs)</li><li>Optical Digitizers and Scanners (ODSs)</li><li>Optical Profiler and Projector</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical measurement equipment market comprises various types designed for precise measurement and inspection. Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMMs) utilize probes to measure the physical geometrical characteristics of an object. Optical Digitizers and Scanners (ODSs) capture the three-dimensional shapes of objects using laser or light technology. Optical profilers measure surface profiles to determine roughness and texture. Other equipment includes optical micrometers and vision systems, which enhance measurement accuracy across diverse applications in manufacturing and quality control.</p></p>
