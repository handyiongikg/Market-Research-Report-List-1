<p><p>The medical eye patches market is primarily categorized into viscous and inviscid types. Viscous eye patches are designed to adhere closely to the skin, providing a secure fit and effective occlusion for conditions like post-surgery recovery or eye protection from light. Conversely, inviscid eye patches utilize a more fluid-like, lightweight design, allowing for easier application and comfort. Both types serve distinct needs in ophthalmic care, addressing patient comfort and therapeutic requirements effectively.</p></p>
