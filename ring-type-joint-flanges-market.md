<p><p>Ring Type Joint Flanges (RTJ flanges) are critical components in high-pressure piping systems, offering effective sealing in various applications. The market features several types: </p><p>1. **Threaded Flanges** allow for easy connection to pipes with threaded ends, facilitating quick assembly without welding.</p><p>2. **Slip-On Flanges** slide over the pipe end and are welded in place, offering good alignment and ease of use.</p><p>3. **Welding Neck Flanges** provide a tapered neck for smooth transitions and are welded directly to the pipe, ensuring strong, robust connections suitable for high-stress environments.</p></p>
