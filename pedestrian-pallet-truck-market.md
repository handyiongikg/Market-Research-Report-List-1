<p><p>The pedestrian pallet truck market can be categorized into manual and electric types. Manual pallet trucks require operators to manually pump the handle for lifting and maneuvering, making them ideal for low-intensity tasks and small spaces. In contrast, electric pallet trucks are powered by batteries, facilitating easier movement and reducing operator fatigue, making them suitable for larger warehouses and frequent use. Both types serve crucial roles in material handling, optimizing efficiency based on specific operational needs.</p></p>
