<p><p>The enema-based products market can be categorized into four types: </p><p>1. **Cleansing enemas** are primarily used to clear the bowel before procedures or surgeries. </p><p>2. **Carminative enemas** help relieve gas and bloating by expelling trapped air. </p><p>3. **Retention enemas** involve holding the liquid in for a timeframe to allow absorption of medicinal properties. </p><p>4. **Return-flow enemas** are designed to relieve intestinal blockage or constipation by allowing fluid to flow back and forth within the colon. Each type serves distinct therapeutic purposes.</p></p>
