<p><p>Neutropenia drugs are specialized medications used to treat neutropenia, a condition characterized by an abnormally low level of neutrophils, a type of white blood cell essential for fighting infections. These drugs primarily include granulocyte colony-stimulating factors (G-CSF), such as filgrastim and pegfilgrastim, which stimulate the bone marrow to produce more neutrophils. The market for neutropenia drugs is expected to grow significantly, driven by the increasing incidence of cancer, particularly treatments like chemotherapy that can lead to neutropenia.</p><p>Market growth analysis indicates that the Neutropenia Drugs Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period.
