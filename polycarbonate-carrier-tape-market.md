<p><ul><li>Black Conductive</li><li>Transparent Antistatic</li><li>Transparent Non-antistatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polycarbonate carrier tape market is segmented into three main types: Black Conductive, Transparent Antistatic, and Transparent Non-antistatic. Black Conductive tapes are designed for use in electronic applications, providing a conductive path that helps prevent static discharge. Transparent Antistatic tapes offer visibility while protecting sensitive components from static electricity, making them ideal for various electronic packaging. Transparent Non-antistatic tapes, on the other hand, are suitable for general applications where static protection is not a critical factor, ensuring versatility in use.</p></p>
