<p><p>Inventory tank gauging systems are crucial for managing storage tanks in various industries. The market is divided into two main types: point level instruments and continuous level instruments. Point level instruments detect specific levels, signaling when a tank is full or empty, providing discrete measurement. In contrast, continuous level instruments offer real-time monitoring of fluid levels, delivering ongoing data for efficient inventory management. Both types enhance operational safety and efficiency by minimizing risks and ensuring accurate stock levels.</p></p>
